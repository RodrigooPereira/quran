بِسۡمِ Bis'mi In (the) name ٱللَّهِ al-lahi of Allah ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ al-rahmani the Most Gracious ٱلرَّحِيمِ al-rahimi the Most Merciful

Bis'mi al-lahi al-rahmani al-rahimi

In the Name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

ٱلۡحَمۡدُ Al-hamdu All praises and thanks لِلَّهِ lillahi (be) to Allah رَبِّ rabbi the Lord ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ al-'alamina of the universe

Al-hamdu lillahi rabbi al-'alamina

All the praises and thanks be to Allah, the Lord of all worlds (mankind, jinns and all that exists)

ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ Al-rahmani the Most Gracious ٱلرَّحِيمِ al-rahimi the Most Merciful

Al-rahmani al-rahimi

The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

مَٰلِكِ Maliki (The) Master يَوۡمِ yawmi (of the) Day ٱلدِّينِ al-dini (of the) Judgment

Maliki yawmi al-dini

Master of the Day of Judgment.

إِيَّاكَ Iyyaka You Alone نَعۡبُدُ na'budu we worship وَإِيَّاكَ wa-iyyaka and You Alone نَسۡتَعِينُ nasta'inu we ask for help

Iyyaka na'budu wa-iyyaka nasta'inu

You (Alone) we worship, and you (Alone) we ask for help

ٱهۡدِنَا Ih'dina Guide us ٱلصِّرَٰطَ al-sirata (to) the path ٱلۡمُسۡتَقِيمَ al-mus'taqima the straight

Ih'dina al-sirata al-mus'taqima

Guide us to the Straight Path

صِرَٰطَ Sirata (The) path ٱلَّذِينَ alladhina (of) those أَنۡعَمۡتَ an'amta You have bestowed (Your) Favors عَلَيۡهِمۡ alayhim on them غَيۡرِ ghayri not (of) ٱلۡمَغۡضُوبِ al-maghdubi those who earned (Your) wrath عَلَيۡهِمۡ alayhim on themselves وَلَا wala and not ٱلضَّآلِّينَ al-dalina (of) those who go astray

Sirata alladhina an'amta alayhim ghayri al-maghdubi alayhim wala al-dalina

the Path of those You have blessed, not those You are displeased with, or those who are astray.
